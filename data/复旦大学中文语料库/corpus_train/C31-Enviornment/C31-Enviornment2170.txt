偶氮染料生产废水的处理工艺技术研究
李庄　 曾光明　 高兴斋　 
摘　要：偶氮染料废水具有高CODCr、高色度、有机物成分复杂等特点。根据清污分流的原则，确定处理工艺方案为：首先对高浓度偶氮染料工艺废水采用中和―电解―臭氧氧化进行预处理后，再与低浓度废水按比例混合混凝处理。经大量工艺实验确定全流程工艺条件为：废水pH中性、电解电压10V、电流密度0.018A/cm2、电解时间2h,O3氧化时间为2h，混凝剂环保1#和3#浓度各为250mg/L。CODCr、色度的去除率分别可以达到99.6%，99.9%。可以达标排放。
关键词：偶氮染料；废水处理；电解；O3氧化；混凝

Study on the Treatment Process Technique of Wastewater from Azo Dye Production
LI Zhuang（Department of Environment Engineering and Sciences of Hunan University, Changsha 410082, China）　
ZENG Guang-ming（Department of Environment Engineering and Sciences of Hunan University, Changsha 410082, China）　
GAO Xing-zhai（Institute of Environment Protection Science of Hunan Province, Changsha 410004, China）　

Abstract：he wastewater of azo dye is characterized with high CODCr,high colority and complication of organic components. According to the characteristic of the wastewater, the high concentration wastewater of azo dye was firstly pre-treated by neutralization-electrolysis-ozonization process,then was compounded with low concentration wastewater in proportion,and treated by coagulating process.The whole process optimum conditions were determined as follows:pH of neutrality,10 V of electrolysis voltage,0.018 A/cm2 of electrical current density,2 h of ozonization, 250 mg/L of environment-protecting coagulant 1#,3#.The removal rates of CODCr and colority are 99.6% and 99.9% respectively. The effluent quality can reach requirements of wastewater discharge standards.
Keywords：azo dye; wastewater treatment; electrolysis; ozonization; coagulant
