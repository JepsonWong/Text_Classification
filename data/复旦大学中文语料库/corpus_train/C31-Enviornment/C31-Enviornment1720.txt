油污土壤微生物处理技术
金文标　 宋莉晖　 吴东平　 范金蔚　 
摘　要：对油污土壤进行微生物处理时，油污土壤中微生物种群和数量、营养物种类和数量对处理效果均有影响。同时向油污土壤中投加高效原油降解菌和优良营养物是最为有效的处理方式。菌剂投加量≥6mL/kg，营养物投加量为使土壤中C∶N=40∶1时为宜。
关键词：油污土壤；　降解菌；　营养物；　地耕
分类号：X741　文献标识码：A

The Technology of Microbe Treatment for Oily Soil
JIN Wen-biao（Environmental Monitoring Central Station,CNPC,Langfang 065007, China）　
SONG Li-hui（Environmental Monitoring Central Station,CNPC,Langfang 065007, China）　
WU Dong-ping（Environmental Monitoring Central Station,CNPC,Langfang 065007, China）　
FAN Jin-wei（Environmental Monitoring Central Station,CNPC,Langfang 065007, China）　
Abstract：Microbe species and amount,and nutrient sorts and concentration all have effects on treatment efficiency in the microbe treatment of oily soil.Adding effcient crude-oil degrading bacteria and optimum nutrient to oily soil at the same time is the most efficient treating mode.The optimum adding amount of bacteria is 6mL/kg or more,and the optimum adding of nutrient is made in case of C∶N=40∶1.
Keywords：oily soil;　degrading bacteria;　nutrient;　tilling
