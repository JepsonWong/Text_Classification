小卫星故障诊断与处理技术
廖明宏　 吴翔虎　 程光明　 
摘　要：介绍一种简单有效的故障诊断及处理技术。它将小卫星的故障分为分系统级、系统级和系统外部级三种，并由星务管理软件处理系统级故障。在每个循环周期都对遥测参数进行采集和监测，当故障发生时，根据故障的优先级，选择并恢复具有最高优先级的故障。该技术在某小卫星中应用，能有效满足系统控制周期的限制和系统可靠性要求。
关键词：小卫星；可靠性；故障检测；故障隔离；故障恢复

Fault Detection Isolation and Recovery for the Small Satellite
LIAO Ming-hong（Computer Science Department of Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin Heilongjiang 150001, China）　
WU Xiang-hu（Computer Science Department of Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin Heilongjiang 150001, China）　
CHENG Guang-ming（Computer Science Department of Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin Heilongjiang 150001, China）　
Abstract：A simple but effective fault diagnosis and processing technique is introduced. The failures of small satellite are divided into three levels: sub-system level, system level and outside system level, and the failures in system level are processed by housekeeping software. The telemetric parameters are sampled and monitored in each control cycle. When failure occurred, only the highest priority failure is selected and recovered. This technique is applied in small satellite, and satisfies the limitation of control cycle and reliability requirement of system.
Keywords：Small satellite; Reliability; Fault detection; Fault isolation; Fault recovery
