A Survey on the Effectiveness of the Treatment for the Cyclist Suffering from Anaemia with Chinese Medical Nutritious Agent
Zhang Chunlin
Abstract:The article adopts critical value termed for anaemia stipulated by WHO, that is for female with (Hb)<120g/l and for male with (Hb)<130g/l as the criteria for diagnosis of anaemia by motion. The author prescribed a Chinese medical nutritious agent for Fujian provincial female cyclist to take in during the winter training and made a tracing survey. By the unit_test of lab, the author found that the Hb value in those cyclist in the period of taking agent under the case with no more nutritious foodstuff added were increased in different scale, no matter how they suffered from anaemia or not. The effectiveness on the treatment for cyclist who suffered from the anaemia was significant. By comparing the unit_test data in different period, the effectiveness on the treatment was stable. No side effect appeared.
