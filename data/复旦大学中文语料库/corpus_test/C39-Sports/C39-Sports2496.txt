Discussion on the Reform of Physical Education Courses and Students
Consciousness of Physical Education
Weng Yongliang
Abstract:In order to reform the physical education courses and to improve the students' fitness, we must study and master the student's psychological basis and to justify their learning attitude with modern concept of physical education. In this paper, the author carries out the experiment, unit_test and comparison analysis according to the reform program by using the form of questionnaire. The main purpose is to bring the training function of physical education into fully play in order to improve the student's fitness and mental health.
