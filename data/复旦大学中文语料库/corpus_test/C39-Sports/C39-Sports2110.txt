Indirect Measurement of Anaerobic Capacities in Humans
Wang Jian��Hong Feng
Abstract:Anaerobic capacity(AC) is defined as the maximal amount of ATP resynthesised via anaerobic metabolism during exercise, Measurement of AC in humans have important applications in the evaluation of human work capacity�� efficiency and training effects.This review focuses on the laboratory dynamic and physiological measures including critical power unit_test, all-out exercise unit_test, peak post-exercise blood lactate method and maximal accumulated oxygen deficit method.A brief discussion focus on the applications of above methods in the sports science research and practice was made in this paper.
