Physical Activities and Healthy Mental Development
of Youth-A Survey and Comparative Study of the
Inducement of Mental Pressure among Youth in Beijing and Hong Kong
Ding Xueqin et al ����������������������1998,18(5):83
(National Research Institute of Sports Science)
Abstract����After analysis of a questionnaire unit_test among 2009 young students in Beijing and Hong Kong, it was found that disturbances caused by mental stress existed commonly among youth in these two cities. The main inducements of these mental pressures were examination, study, personal future, family and money etc. Comparative analysis showed that mental disturbance of youth in Hong Kong was slight heavier than that in Beijing. For those participating physical activities regularly, they were less affected by mental problems than those who did not participate physical activities regularly. It was concluded that regular participation of physical activities was benefit to healthy mental development of youth.
