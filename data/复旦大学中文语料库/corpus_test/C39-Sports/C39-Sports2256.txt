A Hemodynamic Study of Chinese Elite Gymnasts
Yu Jiying et al
1998��18(6):51
(Shanghai Institute of Physical Education, Shanghai, China��200438)
Abstract��136 elite Chinese Gymnasts received hemodynamic examination by CF-II intelligent system of cardiovascular function. Comparison was then made on the difference of hemodynamic parameters between elite gymnasts and the control group in light of the functions of heart, blood vessel, and peripheral and internal circulation. As a result significant hemodynamic parameters were put forward for the comprehensive assessment of the selection and training of top level gymnasts.
