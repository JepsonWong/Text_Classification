THE SOFT FAULT DETECTING AND LSOLATING TO 
ENSURE THE ENTIRE ATTITUDE MEASURING OF 
A STRAPDOWN INERTIAL NAVIGATION SYSTEM
Qin Yongyuan Niu Huifang
(Dept.of Automatic Control Northwestern Polytechnical University.Xi'an.710072)
����Abstract��In the federated filter design,the reliability of a strapdown inertial navigation system should be guaranteed absolutely because it is used as a common reference system.The paper investigates the way to detect and to isolate at most two faulty inertial components.The soft fault detection algorithm based on chi-square unit_test is reviewed.The parity judgement function to isolate two faulty inertial components based on maximun likelyhood unit_test is deduced in detail.The algorithm to search the faulty components is induced.The fault detection and isolation of the scewed redundant ring laser gyro cluster is simulated on the computer.The results show that the searching scheme can detect and isolate faulty components correctly even if the soft faults are as tiny as the gyro drifts.
