FY-1C极轨气象卫星的进展
孟执中　 
摘　要：在概述美国和俄罗斯等国外极轨气象卫星发展概况的基础上，介绍了中国极轨与静止轨道两种气象卫星的业务应用卫星体系，重点介绍了“风云一号”（02星）即FY-1C极轨气象卫星的基本任务、总体设计、技术特点、关键技术以及所取得的进展。最后简述了新一代极轨气象卫星FY-3的发展设想。
关键词：极轨气象卫星；应用卫星；卫星应用；遥感卫星
分类号：P414+.2　文献标识码：A

The Development of FY-1C Polar-Meteorological Satellite
MENG Zhi-zhong（SAST,Shanghai 200233，China）　

Abstract：Based the summary of foreign polar-orbiting weather satellites develo p ment, such as United states, Russia etc. , this paper introduces the service app lication satellite system of China polar-orbiting weather satellites and geost a tionary orbiting weather satellites. The basic function, global design, technique characteristic, key techniques and related development of FY-1C Satellite are stressed. At the end of this paper, the development assumption of new generation polar-orbiting satellite, FY-3 Satellite, is simply related.
Keywords：Polar-orbiting weather satellite；Application satellite； Satellite applications ；Remote sensing satellite
